About Us

Enterprise expertise,
built for everyone.

Built on wisdom. Delivered with care.

arif is personal — it's a piece of my name, Arifin. But it also means wise in Bahasa Malaysia and Arabic. That double meaning captures exactly what I'm here to build.

I spent 13 years as a data scientist and optimisation specialist at PETRONAS and ExxonMobil — building AI-powered systems that global energy companies run on. High-stakes, complex, and genuinely impactful work at scale.

What I kept seeing was the gap. The same capability optimising billion-dollar operations wasn't reaching the business owner down the street — the F&B entrepreneur, the logistics SME, the independent professional who could transform their business with the right AI, but had no way in.

arifAI Solutions exists to close that gap. Enterprise-grade thinking, without the enterprise price tag or complexity — for businesses in Melaka, across Malaysia, and beyond.
